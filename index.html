Ch 01: What Is Democarcy? Why Democracy?

    1.1 Meaning Of Democarcy

  • Rulers are elected by the people to make major decisions
  • Though elections, a fair and choice of oppurtunity is given to people to change their current ruler
  • This choice and oppurtunity is available to all the poeple on an equal basis.
  • The exercise of this choice leads to the formation of the governement, limited by basic rules of the constitution and citizen's rights
  • Different decisions of the government are taken in an open manner, debate in media, invitation to expert's opinion, representations by the common man form a part of decision making process.
  • Citizens have the right to oppose any government policy or activity
